Bio

Hello, I'm Jayme! I am a first year master's student studying computer science at Tufts University. I grew up in the Ballard neighborhood of Seattle, Washington and received my undergraduate degree from Bowdoin College in 2007, double-majoring in physics and classics. At Bowdoin, I played four years of NCAA Division III women's ice hockey and softball.

When I'm not at the keyboard hacking on code, I'm generally running around in the terrible New England weather training for a marathon (or two), plotting my next travel plans, or pining over tiny houses.

You can also listen to my radio show every Monday morning from 7-8am on Tufts' freeform radio station, wmfo.org!
